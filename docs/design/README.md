## Project Information

- **Product**: Easy Risk Register
- **Purpose**: Lightweight risk management tool for SMBs
- **Target Users**: Risk managers and business owners (age 30-50)
- **Core Values**: Simplicity, privacy, accessibility, and effectiveness
